Governance Onboarding Process

After registration, you'll need to complete the following steps:

  1. Invite Required Roles: Send invitations to:
    • Superintendent Pharmacist
    • Supervising Pharmacist
  2. Role Confirmations: Each role must confirm their responsibilities via secure email link
  3. Send Documents: After all roles confirm, email verification documents to admin@terasync.ng
    Required Documents:
    • PCN Registration Certificate
    • CAC Registration Document
    • Pharmacist License
    • Government-Issued ID
    • Proof of Premises
  4. Admin Review: Our team will review and activate your pharmacy

Once all roles are confirmed, you can access the Pharmacy Team section to invite team members and track progress.
